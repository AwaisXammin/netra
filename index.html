What is IDP?

IDP (Intelligent Document Processing) has become a workhorse of today’s industrious world. Extracting data from a large variety of documents and feeding them reliably into the organization’s database has revolutionized our workflows. However, many mid-size and smaller companies are still struggling with the introduction of IDP, as licensing models of the main players exceed their budget and deployment of their large-scale solutions is often complicated and cost-prohibitive.

NETRA is revolutionizing the market by offering a solution that makes IDP affordable to everyone and with a minimum of expertise on the customer’s side. How could we possibly do that? Many traditional IDP solutions are bloated with features only a small percentage of users need. NETRA’s solution is down-to-earth and yet very powerful as we don’t burden the client with building models and learning IDP jargon.

The Truth About IDP

Today’s IDP solutions are very powerful and loaded with features like AI (artificial intelligence), hand writing recognition, image processing and more. With today’s compute power, almost anything is possible. But do we need AI (artificial intelligence) to find the data-of-birth field on a form? In fact, 99 percent of all document processing makes use of image processing tools, but are not “intelligent” in any way and don’t need to be.

All an effective IDP system needs for functioning is a solid recipe of how to find the extractable data on a sheet and where to put them. The formulation of this recipe requires an extensive amount of software flexibility and user expertise. That is why most IDP systems come with an overwhelmingly large user interface layer, which again comes with a whole new world of terminology. They require the client’s personnel to acquire an incredible amount of expertise. This is almost like teaching every airline passenger how to fly, instead of placing two pilots up front.

NETRA’s approach is entirely different. Our systems do not come with a cockpit-like user interface, but just as a toolkit of highly efficient modules. And we provide with it the ‘pilots’ that write the recipe for you on how those modules should work together. We call that recipe a model. And the best thing is:

The model comes for free!
In a nutshell, NETRA’s IDP solution does not require you to hire specialists to build models, has affordable license costs, and can easily be tied into your current IT structure. Too good to be true? Turn the page to see how we can do it.

How it Works

Every IDP project is different, but there is a common blueprint that underlies all projects. Starting an IDP project with Netra is especially easy.

Inquiry

You inquire about a model for a particular application. Either we have that model already in stock, in which case we continue with phase Model Evaluation or we will develop a tailor-made model for you. In that phase we also discuss where your operational data is sourced from and where the extracted data will be placed.

Training Set Preparation

You send us a set of sample documents that we call the training set. As preparation for the development of a model, this training set needs to be annotated, that is we manually determine the desired output data for each document. The training set should have a reasonable size, so the model we build is prepared for all those variations that occur in real-world documents.

Model Training

We will develop the new model and use automated training mechanisms to fine-tune the model accordingly. This step is a combination of software development and machine learning. The development part includes telling the model where to find the various pieces of information. The training will prepare the model for any kind of variations in format and placement of fields.

Model Evaluation

You send us a second set of sample documents, which again will be annotated. We call this the evaluation set. This set is used to verify that the model works as expected, indepen¬dently from the training set. The annotation allows an automated check of the model’s correctness.

Deployment

The model will be deployed at your premises and on your own hardware. Of course, we will support you during this phase to get things running as seamlessly and quickly as possible. We do not require your personnel to become IDP specialists or do weeks of training and preparations.

Maintenance

The world is in motion and so are your documents. Changes do occur over time. And those changes sometimes have to be reflected in the IDP software. We will stand by your side, should any changes become necessary, be it to the model or the embedding in your infrastructure.
