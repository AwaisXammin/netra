What is IDP & Problem Statement

Intelligent Document Processing really is and why EVERY organization needs IDP in today's world. However, the current approach for IDP is flawed, it requires too much upfront investment and resources from organizations in its current form. Hence why the IDP market is mainly geared towards large organizaitons. Our solution changes that dynamic by providing "customized, ready-made solutions" that can be consumed immediately by organizaitons without having to 'wait' to realize value (i.e. having to build model, deploy etc...). We are 'commoditinzg IDP' with our framework, enabling smaller organizations to get the primary benefits of IDP (extracting data from documents and reducing manual effort) without having to spend extra on features that are not a priority to many SMBs (I.e. Keyword analytics or "fancy UIs that supposedly enables 'regular users' to create enterprise models when in reality, they're not going to be as proficient as actual data scientists).

Our Approach

Netra is fundamentally different than almost any otherIDP provider. We don’t have a "UI" or any application that a "business user" can use to build a model. Our platform is made up of modules of code that perform specific functions that streamline the process of creating enterprise-grade IDP models that can work on any use case/document. Thus, you have to be a data scientist or programmer to be able to build one with our platform (a bottleneck, yes. However, we are trying to lean into the point that "data scientists should be building models, and by trying to create a 'layer' on top your technology that helps non-data scientists try to build models only makes the overall process more inefficienct and expensie), however the BUILDING BLOCKS OF CODE in our platform and the WAY WE IMPLEMENT THEM are the key differentiators to use.

Our Solution

Our solution is trained by our data scientists, using our GPUs and resources (instead of the clients who have to build them and spend money on AWS/Azure etc..) so our clients can deploy them in ANY ENVIRONMENT, without requiring even an internet connection. It can directly integrate into our clients workflows and extract data from documents that are sourced from any location (in a DOucment Management System, Email, Share drives etc...) and extract the necessary data and send the data to any application/location.

Due to our approach, we are able to provide pricing at a level, no one can come close to. Most IDP providers charge based on "PEr Page fees" or "Project Fees" plus "Annual Licensing Costs" etc...causing it to be confusing for many potential customers, expecially those with limited budget. Our solution once trained doesn't require anything further from the customer, we just integrate the model into their environment and that's it unless there are any updates to be made. Thus, the normal IDP price range starts at $50,000 or more just to START and that can go up significantly dpeending on page requirements etc......in our attempt to 'commoditize IDP', we are changing the pricing model to be $1/Day, unlimited documents.

Our Values

Our Values are based around the idea that IDP as a concept is simple, you are extracting data from a document and taking that data and putting it somewhere. Sure, there are many different types of documents (Structured, Semi-structured, Unstructured, Dimensional) with their own complexities (i.e. handwritten text etc...)...but the VAST majority of documents DO NOT NEED fancy features, they need barebones functionality that deliver on what it's supposed to do...just like a commodity. We value in being transparent and not to 'oversell' our importance...we're not changing the world or trying to. We are just trying to make it so simple for SMBs to leverage IDP that it can turn into the 'commodity' it really is.

Why Are We Building NETRA

We believe that organizations of any size should be able to leverage the benefits of IDP, we don't believe organizations should have to pay based on the amount of documents they need to process or have to pay licensing fees every year. We are trying to again 'commoditize' the IDP space, the automation technology called 'RPA' has become 'commoditized' because when you compare the Top 3 Vendors (Uipath, Automaiton Anywhrere, Blue Prism) SOLELY upon their RPA technology (not their otherproduts within their suite like "Document Understanding" from Uipath which is IDP) they are essentially a 'wash'. They all have pretty much the same capabilities and of course they do, they all do the same thing...they automate repetitive, common tasks...sure, some of them are able to do fancier things but that goes OUTSIDE the bounds of RPA. The same concept applies to IDP, we are extracting data from documents and putting them somewhere, nothing more. All capabilities who add "advanced analytics" or "Workflow automation" on top of it are just trying to add functionality to their fundamental capability, which is again, being able to extract data.

Our Plan To Commoditize IDP

Our framework and approach of models not to focus on "use cases" (like build a model for Invoices) but rather on fundamental components (an invoice will always have a 'table' within it that outlines the line items being bought...we have a 'Table extractor model' that can automate the extration of that table from any invoice...but it can also work on a medical claim which ALSO has a table in it..just with different data. We are approaching this more fundamentally than other providers and want to highlight in here that our plan once we scale is to have a 'library' of "ready-made" solutions that organizations no matter how small can just buy and add to their automation program. So imagine small businesses who want to move into a new service line (i.e. Mortgage lending...but currently you only do personal lending, you can already have all 1003 mortgage application data is automatically extracted and transferred to Origination system for an underwriter to review) theyu could leverage netra to address a core fundamental part of businesses, data extraction. We provide a mechanism to offer it at a price no one else can match because they already 'sold their soul' (so to speka) to Venture Capital funds at big valuations and sub-optimal frameworks.

How it Works

We have "ready-made" models for specific use cases like 'Drivers License/Identity Documents' or "Signature Identification", but then we have our "Core Models" like "Table Extractor" or "Para" or "Signature Idenitfication" models which serve as 'components' that are applied to different use cases. Please refer to GTM sheet on excel file to see from a customer perspective, they either need one or all of our ready-made models and then we can build new models for other documents using our 'Netra framework (the core models like Table Extractor), all we would need is to acquire their data (the amount of sample vary based on model complexity) and build out model and then integrate into the clients workflow within 4 weeks* (depending on project scope).
