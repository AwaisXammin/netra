About Us

We started Netra with the objective to deliver outstanding business process improvements using intelligent automation. Our goal is to democratize the usage of IDP solutions by providing 100% secure, highly accurate and cost-effective on-premises IDP solutions. And all that at the fraction of the traditional costs. At a fixed rate of 1$ /day/model/user the ROI Netra delivers is unmatched industry-wide.

We believe that organizations of any size should be able to leverage the benefits of IDP, we don't believe organizations should have to pay based on the amount of documents they need to process or have to pay licensing fees every year.

Our framework and modeling techniques don’t focus on specific applications (like e.g. reading invoices) but rather on fundamental building blocks. For example, an invoice will always have a table within it that outlines the line items being bought; hence we have a Table Extractor model, which can be applied across a wide range of applications.
