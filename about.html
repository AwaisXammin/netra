Our Values

Our Values are based around the idea that IDP as a concept is simple, you are extracting data from a document and taking that data and putting it somewhere. Sure, there are many different types of documents (Structured, Semi-structured, Unstructured, Dimensional) with their own complexities (i.e. handwritten text etc...)...but the VAST majority of documents DO NOT NEED fancy features, they need barebones functionality that deliver on what it's supposed to do...just like a commodity. We value in being transparent and not to 'oversell' our importance...we're not changing the world or trying to. We are just trying to make it so simple for SMBs to leverage IDP that it can turn into the 'commodity' it really is.
