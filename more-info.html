What is IDP?

ㅤIDP (Intelligent Document Processing) has become a workhorse of today’s industrious world. Extracting data from a large variety of documents and feeding them reliably into the organization’s database has revolutionized our workflows. However, many mid-size and smaller companies are still struggling with the introduction of IDP, as licensing models of the main players exceed their budget and deployment of their large-scale solutions is often complicated and cost-prohibitive.

ㅤ NETRA is revolutionizing the market by offering a solution that makes IDP affordable to everyone and with a minimum of expertise on the customer’s side. How could we possibly do that? Many traditional IDP solutions are bloated with features only a small percentage of users need. NETRA’s solution is down-to-earth and yet very powerful as we don’t burden the client with building models and learning IDP jargon.

The Truth About IDP

Today’s IDP solutions are very powerful and loaded with features like AI (artificial intelligence), hand writing recognition, image processing and more. With today’s compute power, almost anything is possible. But do we need AI (artificial intelligence) to find the data-of-birth field on a form? In fact, 99 percent of all document processing makes use of image processing tools, but are not “intelligent” in any way and don’t need to be.

All an effective IDP system needs for functioning is a solid recipe of how to find the extractable data on a sheet and where to put them. The formulation of this recipe requires an extensive amount of software flexibility and user expertise. That is why most IDP systems come with an overwhelmingly large user interface layer, which again comes with a whole new world of terminology. They require the client’s personnel to acquire an incredible amount of expertise. This is almost like teaching every airline passenger how to fly, instead of placing two pilots up front.

NETRA’s approach is entirely different. Our systems do not come with a cockpit-like user interface, but just as a toolkit of highly efficient modules. And we provide with it the ‘pilots’ that write the recipe for you on how those modules should work together. We call that recipe a model. And the best thing is:

The model comes for free!
In a nutshell, NETRA’s IDP solution does not require you to hire specialists to build models, has affordable license costs, and can easily be tied into your current IT structure. Too good to be true? Turn the page to see how we can do it.

Netra’s Powerhouse Modules

There are two disciplines that lie at the core of every IDP project:

Locating the desired content

Locating the content often means recognizing a table structure on paper and finding the table cells. Easy if the table has grid of lines, but difficult if those lines are missing.

Netra’s table extractor module is prepared for tables of all types, with and without grids, with colored backgrounds, nested tables, and more.

Converting it to data, that is: into a string of character codes.

Once the content has been located it has to be converted to a character string, which is what our OCR module does.

Netra uses its own OCR engine, which is superior to what you find generally in the market.

The quality of the OCR engine is crucial for achieving reliable results. Hence, we have optimized our OCR in many different ways.

Those are just two examples of the Netra modules from which we build highly reliable solutions.

Most of our modules use deep learning techniques to be easily trainable to new source material.

At the end of the day, it’s the quality of the modules that determines the quality of an IDP solution and that’s where Netra’s focus is.
